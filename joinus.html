Join us

We welcome enthusiastic and collaborative researchers!

Our research group consists of multiple MSc and PhD students and postdocs, but we always welcome international researchers.

STUDENT INTERNSHIP

We welcome students who would like to stay among us learning techniques and procedures while giving us a hand in developing our research.

PHD STUDENT

We open PhD scholarships once in a while, usually linked to funded projects. We will broadly advertise any time a position is available.

PHD VISITING FELLOW

We also welcome PhD students from other universities and countries who want to join us for a few months and collaborate in ongoing research activities. 

POSTDOC

We have experience in MSCA and other European funding programs, and we are willing to aid researchers to get grants that will enable them to join our group.
